Lobster recipe

Description

These lobster tails cooked in the air fryer are incredibly tender and delicious.

Topped with a melted buttery garlic herb sauce, this is a quick and easy way to have that delectable fancy dinner without a lot of time and preparation!

Ingredients

  • 4 lobster tails
  • salt and pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary minced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to broil or 500 degrees. Start by preparing the lobster. Using kitchen shears butterfly the tail by cutting down the center.
  2. In a small bowl whisk together the melted butter, garlic, paprika, thyme, rosemary, and parsley.
  3. Broil the lobster for about 8-10 minutes or until the meat is opaque and lightly brown on the top.
  4. Salt and pepper the meat and set on a baking sheet.
