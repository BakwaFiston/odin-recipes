Pancakes recipe

Description

Her homemade bread is seriously one of the best recipes out there

They are so fluffy and perfect every single time!

Ingredients

  • Egg not only adds a boost of protein, but it also helps to bind the ingredients together, resulting in a fluffy and delicious stack of homemade pancakes!
  • Canola oil adds moisture to the batter and ensures that the pancakes do not stick to the pan. You can also use butter or margarine if you had like
  • No pancake recipe would be complete without a generous pour of milk. This ingredient provides the liquid base for the batter, and the fat content helps to keep the pancakes moist and tender
  • A pinch is all you need! It helps to boost the flavors of the other ingredients and balance out the sweetness of the sugar.

Steps

  1. In a medium sized bowl whisk together egg, oil and milk. In a separate bowl combine flour, salt, sugar and baking powder
  2. For leftover batter, cover it with plastic wrap and refrigerate for up to 24 hours just give it a quick stir before using. Pancake prep has never been easier!
  3. The backbone of any good pancake recipe, flour gives the pancakes their structure and texture. Make sure to measure your flour accurately to keep your pancakes from turning out dry and dense.
  4. A pinch is all you need! It helps to boost the flavors of the other ingredients and balance out the sweetness of the sugar.
